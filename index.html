Background

CozyCasa is a contemporary furniture brand founded with a strong belief that there are ways to change the furniture industry and to develop close relationships with customers to ensure that the life cycle of each piece of furniture is as long as possible. “We are fully committed to doing our part in producing a product with the most sustainable materials, processes and shipping methods available on the market, but real change needs to happen in close relationship with the user. The most sustainable products are furniture.

It was used many times and over a long period of time and was eventually recycled with materials that could be reused”. Instead of focusing on sustainable bullshit, CozyCasa focuses on transparency to gain a reputation as the world's most transparent furniture brand. Transparency is ensured through full traceability throughout the supply chain and a “factory information label” that specifies the most important facts about the suppliers involved in the manufacturing process of each piece of furniture. CozyCasa believes that great style and sustainable shopping can go hand in hand.

Our Vision

CozyCasa plans recycling and sustainability for all of its products, meaning that all furniture can be repaired free of charge at one of our local repair partners. In addition, CozyCasa will launch a reuse/recycle program based on a network of local partners. CozyCasa encourages all our customers to hand over their used products to preferred second-hand establishments if the product has been used for a long time and is beyond repair/reuse CozyCasa encourages all customers to recycle their products at one of their preferred local recycling partners. CozyCasa wants to minimize the prices and shipping of products, therefore it has no intention of claiming any product back.
